Our Food

The Hungry Llama opened its doors in March 2023 and has quickly become a beloved spot for food lovers seeking bold flavors and warm hospitality. Behind The Hungry Llama are passionate chefs and hosts with deep roots in the culinary world, bringing years of experience and heart to everything they do. The kitchen is led by Alejandro Rojas, a Bolivian chef known for blending traditional recipes with a modern twist. Alejandro has cooked in some of South America's most vibrant kitchens and brings that energy and soul to every dish. The front of house is managed by Camila Vargas, who ensures every guest feels like family the moment they walk in.

At The Hungry Llama, we don’t limit ourselves to one single cooking style. Our inspiration comes from across Bolivia’s diverse regions — from the Andes to the Amazon — and beyond. We focus on preparing high-quality ingredients in the most flavorful, eye-catching ways possible.

The menu is a celebration of Bolivian cuisine: you’ll find flavorful starters inspired by street markets, charcoal-grilled meats cooked to perfection, fresh fish dishes, and a selection of decadent desserts that you won’t want to miss. Every plate is crafted with care, every drink served with a smile — and every visit leaves you just a little hungrier for more.

The Bar

At The Hungry Llama bar, we take pride in crafting high-quality, house-made cocktails inspired by the rich flavors of Bolivia. We steer clear of artificial colors and flavors, choosing instead to create our own syrups, infusions, and signature blends using natural, authentic ingredients. From handpicked Andean herbs to tropical fruits from the lowlands, every drink is a nod to Bolivia’s diverse regions. Our bar is part of a new wave of cocktail culture — blending tradition and creativity to offer a truly unique drinking experience in the heart of the city.
