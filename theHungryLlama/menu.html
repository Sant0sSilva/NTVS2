Menu

Starters

Empanadas: Golden pastry filled with your choice of chicken, beef, or cheese, served with a side of spicy peanut sauce.

Anticuchos: Grilled skewers of marinated beef heart, accompanied by boiled potatoes and a spicy llajwa sauce.

Salteñas: Savory baked pastries filled with a juicy mixture of meat, potatoes, peas, and olives, seasoned with traditional Bolivian spices.​

Main Courses

Pique Macho: A hearty platter of bite-sized beef, sausages, boiled eggs, and fries, topped with sautéed onions and locoto peppers.

Silpancho: Breaded and fried beef cutlet served over rice and potatoes, topped with a fried egg and fresh tomato salsa. ​

Fricasé: A rich pork stew cooked with yellow chili peppers, garlic, and spices, traditionally served with hominy corn.

Sides

Yuca Frita: Crispy fried cassava sticks, perfect for dipping in a tangy salsa.

Arroz con Queso: Creamy rice cooked with melted cheese, a comforting Bolivian favorite

Papitas: Seasoned and fried small potatoes, offering a delightful crispy texture.

Deserts

Buñuelos: Fluffy fried dough drizzled with syrup or honey, often enjoyed during festive occasions.

Cocadas: Sweet coconut biscuits made with condensed milk, offering a chewy and delightful treat.

Leche Asada: A traditional baked milk custard with a caramelized top, similar to flan but with a unique Bolivian twist.
